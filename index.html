Privacy Policy for Synergyy Tech

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Synergyy Tech. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy applies to all information collected through our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you register and use the app:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name and Email address (collected via Firebase Authentication).
  • Authentication Data: User IDs used to secure your account and track your progress in the "Classes" section.
  • App Usage Data: We track interactions such as which subjects (e.g., Process Engineering, Simulation) are accessed to improve our content.
  • Device Information: Basic device metadata (Model, OS version) required for the video player (ExoPlayer) and Firebase services to function correctly.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide access to educational Videos, Library materials, and PPTs.
  • To facilitate the Expert Discussion chat feature.
  • To notify you about updates (e.g., when the PDF/Image upload feature moves from "Coming Soon" to "Live").

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own policies:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database management, and app analytics.
  • ExoPlayer: For video streaming functionality.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your information as long as your account is active. Users can request data deletion by contacting us at the support email provided below.

6. "Coming Soon" Features

Please note that certain features, such as PDF and Image uploads in the Expert section, are currently under development. No data related to these specific file types is collected until the feature is officially released.
